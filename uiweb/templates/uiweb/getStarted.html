Here are the simple steps to follow
  1. Register on www.sccs.com.
  2. Verify your email to activate your account.
  3. On your tablet, install our mask detection app here.
  4. We will install the devices(motion sensors and thermal camera) in your store.


How it works?

  1. Customer arrives at the store entry.
  2. System detects if customer is wearing a mask using app installed on the tablet and camera.
  3. System checks the body temperature of customer using thermal sensor.
  4. Motion sensor installed at the entrance detects when customer enters and keeps the count of customers in the store.
  5. Motion sensor installed at the exit detects when customer leaves and updates the customer count.

If store has reached the maximum occupancy, customer need to wait for their turn.

  1. Customer will scan the QR which is displayed on the tablet.
  2. Customer can fill the form online and to join the queue.
  3. They may stay in the queue or leave.
  4. To encourage customers to stay in the queue, we display the offers and free credits they get at while they shop your store,if any.
  5. Message will be sent to customers when its their turn to enter the store.
