Smart Customer Control System for Covid 19 allows you to run your business smoothly while maintaining social distance.

Key Features

  • Mask detection
  • Temperature checking
  • Queueing
  • Promotion and Advertisement


You will require a tablet and WiFi connection to use Smart Customer Control System. The tablet diaplays the QR code for the customers to enter the waiting queue. You also need to install our mask detection app on your tablet. Our system detects when someone is entering or leaving the store, checks the temperature of person and detects if person is wearing mask. If a person not wearing mask or having higher temperature than normal is detected, then they they are not allowed to enter the store.
